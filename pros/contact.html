Frequently Asked Questions

Are you the window installation company?

No. We are a marketing service that connects homeowners with local window replacement professionals. If you have questions about a specific quote or appointment, please contact the contractor who reached out to you directly.

How do I cancel my request?

Since we do not manage the appointments, you will need to speak with the service provider who contacted you to cancel or reschedule.

Is this service free?

Yes, using our website to check eligibility and request quotes is 100% free for homeowners.
